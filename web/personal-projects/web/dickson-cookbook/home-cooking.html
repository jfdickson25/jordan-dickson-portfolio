This is Darlene Dickson. The amazing cook that brought so many amazing meals
to our home. She is the best Mother a boy could ask for. So many times I remember
coming to the kitchen just to get some scraps of what was dinner because I was
so excited to eat her food. She will forever be an inpiration to me and this
cookbook is a little help guide so we can create memories for others
like the ones she has created for us.
